FRAUX (n.) Naturalesque hair extensions that are virtually indistinguishable from your own unprocessed hair.

Fraux Hair, is an assortment of high quality kinky, curly textured hair extensions designed to blend with natural textured, chemically unprocessed hair. We provide a variety of super tight curly hair that blends well, doesn't shed like crazy, looks marvelous, and isn't a nightmare to manage! Fraux Hair extensions blend naturally with highly textured, curly and kinky hair providing versatility and protective styling options to those with highly textured, curly and kinky hair.

I've worn and loved the versatility of extensions for many years. For a long time, wearing extensions meant constantly having to straighten the portions of my own hair that were left out to blend with the extensions OR have awful looking mismatched textures. I desired the length, volume and versatility that you can get with extensions but I also wanted something that resembled my own naturally textured hair that I could blend with no heat and little manipulation. In my quest for hair that would blend flawlessly with my own natural hair, Fraux Hair was created.
